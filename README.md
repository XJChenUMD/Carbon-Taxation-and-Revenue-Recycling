# Carbon-Taxation-and-Revenue-Recycling

This project provides the code and results used in the research paper: "A global just transition through carbon taxation and revenue recycling"

Author list:
Xiangjie Chen 1, Daniele Malerba 2,  Kuishuang Feng 3, Yannick Oswald 4, Laixiang Sun 1, Klaus Hubacek 5 

1 Department of Geographical Sciences, University of Maryland, College Park, MD, USA. 2 German Institute of Development and Sustainability (IDOS), Research Programme “Transformation of Economic and Social Systems”, Bonn, Germany. 3 Department of Geography, The University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, China. 4 Institute of Geography and Sustainability, University of Lausanne, Lausanne, Switzerland. 5 Integrated Research on Energy, Environment and Society (IREES), Energy and Sustainability Research Institute Groningen, University of Groningen, Groningen, Netherlands. 

Xiangjie and Daniele contributed equally to this research. 

Corresponding authors: K.F. (kfeng@hku.hk), K.H. (k.hubacek@rug.nl).

The code folder contains all the code used for modelling, analysis, and visualisation in the paper.
